Nucleation Functions Documentation

1. compute_undercooling(T_m, T)

Formula: ΔT = Tₘ − T

Description: Calculates the undercooling as the difference between the melting temperature (Tₘ) and the current temperature (T).

2. compute_volume_energy(delta_T)

Formula: ΔGᵥ = (L * ΔT) / Tₘ

Description: Computes the volume free energy change based on the undercooling, latent heat (L), and melting temperature.

3. compute_critical_radius(delta_Gv)

Formula: r* = (2 * γ) / ΔGᵥ

Description: Calculates the critical radius for nucleation using the surface energy (γ) and volume free energy change.

4. compute_nucleation_barriers(delta_T)

Formula: ΔGₕₒₘₒ = (16 * π * γ³) / (3 * ΔGᵥ²), ΔGₕₑₜₑᵣₒ = f(θ) * ΔGₕₒₘₒ

Description: Computes homogeneous and heterogeneous nucleation barriers, accounting for the contact angle factor f(θ).

5. compute_nucleation_probability(delta_G, T)

Formula: P = exp(-ΔG / (k_B * T))

Description: Calculates the probability of nucleation based on the nucleation barrier and temperature.
